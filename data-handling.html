Data Handling

Last updated: April 13, 2026

All Data Stays on Your Device

Peptide Compass Pro stores all data locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. Ever.

There is no cloud sync, no remote database, no user accounts, and no backend. The app runs entirely in your browser using standard web technologies.

Where Your Data Lives

Your data is stored in your browser's localStorage — a sandboxed storage area that is:

  • Local to your device and browser
  • Not shared across devices
  • Not accessible to any website other than this app
  • Not transmitted over the network

Data stored includes your protocols, injection history, schedule preferences, and app settings.

No Server Communication

Beyond loading the app files themselves on first install, Peptide Compass Pro makes no network requests that involve your data. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to any API or server
  • No analytics or telemetry is collected
  • No background sync or data upload occurs
  • No third-party services receive your health data

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete all app data at any time. There are two ways:

Option 1 — From within the app:

1

Open the app and go to the More tab

2

Tap Reset All Data

3

Confirm the reset — all locally stored data will be cleared

Option 2 — From your browser:

1

Open your browser's Settings or Developer Tools

2

Navigate to Storage or Site Data

3

Find and clear data for this site — this removes all localStorage entries and cached files

Data Portability

You can export your data at any time using the Export Data option in the More tab. This produces a JSON file saved to your device that you can keep as a backup or import into a new device.

To move your data to another device, export from the old device and use Import Data on the new one.

Offline Use

Peptide Compass Pro is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Once installed, it works entirely offline. The service worker caches the app's files on your device — it does not cache or sync your personal data.
