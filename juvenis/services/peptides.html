What We Offer

Our Peptide Protocols

Every peptide is sourced from licensed compounding pharmacies. Every protocol is designed around your specific goals and lab results.

BPC-157
Body Protective Compound
Derived from a naturally occurring protein in the body. BPC-157 is one of the most studied peptides for tissue repair and injury recovery.
  • Tendon and ligament healing
  • Muscle repair and recovery
  • GI tract healing (IBD, gastric ulcers)
  • Reduced inflammation systemically
  • Neurological support
Sermorelin
GHRH Analog
A growth hormone releasing hormone analog that stimulates your pituitary gland to produce more natural growth hormone — with fewer side effects than direct GH therapy.
  • Increased lean muscle mass
  • Reduced body fat
  • Improved sleep quality
  • Better bone density
  • Enhanced mood and cognitive function
Ipamorelin + CJC-1295
GH Releasing Stack
Two peptides commonly used together for a synergistic effect on growth hormone release. CJC-1295's longer half-life means fewer injections with sustained results.
  • Accelerated workout recovery
  • Increased muscle mass and strength
  • Fat loss and improved body composition
  • Better sleep architecture
NAD+
Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide
An essential coenzyme involved in cellular energy production (ATP). NAD+ levels decline with age — replenishing them supports energy, recovery, and longevity.
  • Improved cellular energy and metabolism
  • Reduced muscle fatigue and soreness
  • Faster recovery from intense training
  • Neuroprotective benefits
PT-141
Bremelanotide
Acts on melanocortin receptors in the brain to support sexual arousal and function. Works for both men and women through a central (brain-based) mechanism.
  • Male sexual dysfunction support
  • Female sexual arousal disorder
  • Brain-based mechanism (not vascular)
GAC
Glutamine · Arginine · Carnitine
A targeted amino acid combination that supports performance, recovery, and metabolic function through complementary pathways.
  • Muscle repair and protein synthesis
  • Improved blood flow via nitric oxide
  • Fat as fuel — enhanced endurance
  • Reduced muscle soreness
