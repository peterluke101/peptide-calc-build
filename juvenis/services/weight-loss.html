What We Use

Tools That Actually Move the Needle

Depending on your labs and goals, your protocol may include one or more of the following — each selected based on what your body actually needs.

01

GLP-1 Receptor Agonists

Semaglutide and tirzepatide have changed what's possible in medical weight loss. We prescribe them when appropriate — with full lab support and ongoing monitoring.

02

Hormone Optimization

Low testosterone, thyroid dysfunction, and elevated estrogen are major drivers of fat accumulation and metabolic slowdown. Correcting these changes the game.

03

Metabolic Peptides

Peptides like Ipamorelin and CJC-1295 support growth hormone production, which plays a central role in fat metabolism and lean muscle preservation.

04

Insulin Sensitivity Support

Insulin resistance is the hidden driver behind belly fat and stalled weight loss. We test for it — and address it — directly.

05

NAD+ Therapy

NAD+ supports cellular energy metabolism and mitochondrial function. Replenishing it can meaningfully improve energy levels during caloric restriction.

06

Nutrient Repletion

Deficiencies in magnesium, vitamin D, zinc, and B vitamins impair metabolic function. We test for them and correct them as part of the protocol.
