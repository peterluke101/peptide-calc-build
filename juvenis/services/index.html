Is This Right for You?

Who We Work With

Good Candidates

  • Adults 25–75 experiencing symptoms of hormonal decline
  • People whose standard blood work "comes back normal" but who still feel off
  • Athletes and high performers focused on recovery and longevity
  • Women navigating perimenopause or menopause symptoms
  • Anyone frustrated with conventional medicine's response to their concerns
  • Florida residents or those willing to visit in person
  • People ready to commit to labs, follow-up, and protocol compliance

What to Expect

  • Everything starts with comprehensive bloodwork — no exceptions
  • Free 15-minute consultation before any financial commitment
  • Blood panel fee: $349 (70+ markers, no insurance required)
  • Medications sourced from licensed compounding pharmacies only
  • Ongoing follow-up labs every 6–8 weeks (initial phase)
  • Direct access to Dr. Paul — not a rotating staff of PAs
  • No insurance accepted — no insurance interference either
