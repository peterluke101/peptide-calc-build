What We Test

A Panel That Actually
Tells You Something

Most annual physicals test 10–12 markers. Our comprehensive men's health panel covers 70+ factors — because if you're not looking, you won't find it.

Hormone Panel

Total & free testosterone, DHEA-S, estradiol, LH, FSH, prolactin, SHBG — the full hormonal picture, not just a total T number.

Core

Thyroid Function

TSH, free T3, free T4, and reverse T3. Thyroid issues are commonly missed and directly impact energy, metabolism, and mood.

Core

Metabolic Health

Fasting glucose, insulin, HbA1c, and a full lipid panel with particle sizing. Metabolic dysfunction drives aging faster than almost anything else.

Core

Inflammatory Markers

hs-CRP, homocysteine, ferritin. Chronic low-grade inflammation is invisible without labs — and it silently accelerates every health issue.

Advanced

Nutrient Status

Vitamin D, B12, magnesium, zinc, folate. Deficiencies here cause symptoms that mimic hormone imbalance and are easily corrected.

Advanced

Complete Metabolic Panel

Kidney and liver function, electrolytes, CBC. A full systems check — because optimization means ensuring your foundations are solid.
