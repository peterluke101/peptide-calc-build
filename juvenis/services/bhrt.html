What You May Be Experiencing

These Symptoms Are Real.
And They're Addressable.

Many women are told their symptoms are "just part of aging" or dismissed with antidepressants. Often, the root cause is hormonal — and when balanced correctly, the symptoms resolve.

Perimenopause & Menopause
  • Hot flashes and night sweats
  • Sleep disturbances and insomnia
  • Irregular or heavy periods
  • Vaginal dryness and discomfort
  • Mood swings, anxiety, or depression
  • Brain fog and difficulty concentrating
  • Reduced libido or sexual dysfunction
  • Joint pain and muscle aches
General Hormonal Imbalance
  • Persistent fatigue regardless of sleep
  • Unexplained weight gain — especially midsection
  • Hair thinning or excessive shedding
  • Skin changes — dryness, acne, or dullness
  • Bloating, fluid retention, or breast tenderness
  • Low energy and motivation
  • Thyroid symptoms — cold intolerance, constipation
  • Adrenal fatigue and stress sensitivity
Bioidentical vs. Synthetic

Why We Use Bioidentical
Hormone Therapy

Bioidentical hormones are molecularly identical to the hormones your body produces. Unlike synthetic alternatives, they're compounded to match your exact needs — your labs drive every dose.

Estradiol (E2)
Primary Estrogen

The most active form of estrogen. Declining estradiol drives hot flashes, vaginal atrophy, bone loss, and cardiovascular risk. Restoring it properly provides significant protective benefits.

Progesterone
Balance & Protective

Bioidentical progesterone balances estrogen, improves sleep quality, and supports mood and anxiety. Essential for women who still have a uterus — and beneficial for most others.

Testosterone
Libido, Energy & Cognition

Women produce testosterone too — and low levels cause low libido, fatigue, poor body composition, and cognitive decline. A small, properly dosed amount makes a substantial difference.

Thyroid (T3 & T4)
Metabolism & Energy

Thyroid dysfunction affects roughly 1 in 8 women. We test beyond TSH alone — measuring free T3, free T4, and reverse T3 to find dysfunction that standard panels miss.

DHEA
Adrenal & Vitality

A precursor to both estrogen and testosterone. DHEA declines sharply with age and chronic stress. Restoring optimal levels supports energy, libido, immune function, and mood.

Cortisol & Adrenals
Stress & Recovery

Chronic adrenal stress depletes hormones at every level. We assess adrenal function and address burnout patterns that interfere with hormone balance — and overall quality of life.

Important: Hormone therapy is not appropriate for everyone. Our evaluation process is designed to identify candidates who will benefit most and screen for contraindications. All protocols are supervised and adjusted based on regular follow-up labs.
