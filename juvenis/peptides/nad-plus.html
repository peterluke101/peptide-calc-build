Benefits

What NAD+ Therapy Does

🔋 Cellular Energy Production

NAD+ is essential for the electron transport chain — the process that converts nutrients into ATP (cellular energy). More NAD+ means more efficient energy production at the mitochondrial level.

🧬 DNA Repair

PARP enzymes that repair daily DNA damage require NAD+ as a substrate. Adequate NAD+ supports the cellular repair processes that protect against aging and cellular dysfunction.

🧠 Mental Clarity & Focus

The brain is one of the most metabolically active organs. NAD+ supplementation frequently produces noticeable improvements in cognitive function, focus, and mood within days of starting.

💪 Physical Performance & Recovery

NAD+ supports mitochondrial biogenesis and reduces oxidative stress from exercise. Athletes report faster recovery, improved endurance, and better training adaptation.

⚖️ Metabolic Function

NAD+ activates sirtuins that regulate glucose metabolism and fat burning. Restoring NAD+ can improve insulin sensitivity and support healthy weight management.

🛡️ Inflammation & Longevity

Sirtuin activation — which requires NAD+ — modulates inflammatory pathways and is central to multiple longevity mechanisms studied in aging research.

FAQ

NAD+ — Frequently Asked Questions

What is NAD+ and why does it matter?

NAD+ (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in every cell, essential for energy metabolism, DNA repair, sirtuin activation, and hundreds of enzymatic reactions. Levels decline significantly with age — by up to 50% between 40 and 60 — and this decline is directly linked to many hallmarks of aging.

Is NAD+ therapy available via telehealth?

Yes. NAD+ is a non-controlled compound available nationwide. Juvenis Medical prescribes subcutaneous NAD+ to patients in all 50 states via telehealth — delivered to your home, no clinic visit required.

How quickly does NAD+ work?

Many patients notice improved energy and mental clarity within the first week, especially during a loading phase. Sleep quality and sustained energy improvements typically become apparent at 3–6 weeks. The deeper longevity and metabolic benefits develop over months of consistent use.

Can I take NAD+ precursors (NMN, NR) instead?

Oral NMN and NR can raise NAD+ levels, but injectable NAD+ bypasses the conversion steps and reaches circulation more efficiently. For patients seeking clinically meaningful effects, injectable NAD+ typically outperforms oral precursors.

What does NAD+ combine well with?

NAD+ pairs exceptionally well with growth hormone peptides (Sermorelin, Ipamorelin/CJC-1295) and BPC-157 as part of a comprehensive anti-aging protocol. It's also commonly combined with resveratrol, which potentiates sirtuin activation.
