What It Does

Restore Your Body's Natural
Growth Hormone Cycle

Growth hormone (GH) peaks in your 20s and declines roughly 14% per decade after that. By your 40s, most people have lost more than half their youthful GH output — and they feel it. Sermorelin doesn't replace GH artificially. It tells your pituitary gland to start producing it again, the way it's supposed to.

Deep, Restorative Sleep

GH is primarily released during deep sleep. Restoring GH production dramatically improves sleep architecture and morning recovery.

Lean Muscle Preservation

GH is anabolic — it drives protein synthesis and muscle repair. Higher GH levels make it easier to build and hold onto lean tissue.

Fat Metabolism

GH activates lipolysis — the breakdown of stored fat for energy. Restoring GH levels helps shift body composition toward less fat, more muscle.

Faster Recovery

Athletes and active adults notice dramatically faster recovery from training when GH production is optimized — less soreness, better next-day performance.

Skin & Hair Quality

GH promotes collagen synthesis and cellular turnover. Patients often report improved skin texture, elasticity, and hair quality within months.

Cognitive Function

GH receptors are present in the brain. Adequate GH levels support mental clarity, focus, and mood — areas that decline noticeably with GH deficiency.

Immune Support

GH plays a regulatory role in immune function. Optimal levels support immune resilience, especially important as natural production declines with age.

Bone Density

GH stimulates IGF-1, which is critical for bone metabolism. Long-term GH support may help preserve bone density and reduce osteoporosis risk.

FAQ

Sermorelin — Frequently Asked Questions

What is sermorelin and how is it different from HGH?

Sermorelin is a growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) analog that stimulates your pituitary gland to produce its own GH naturally. Unlike synthetic HGH injections, sermorelin restores your body's own production cycle — resulting in more physiologic hormone levels with a significantly better safety profile.

Is sermorelin available via telehealth?

Yes. Sermorelin is a non-controlled peptide available to patients in all 50 states via telehealth. You don't need to be a Florida resident — just a valid prescription and a licensed pharmacy to fill it.

When will I start seeing results?

Most patients notice improved sleep quality within the first 2–4 weeks. Body composition changes, energy improvements, and recovery benefits typically emerge at 8–12 weeks. Full optimization often takes 3–6 months of consistent use.

How is sermorelin administered?

Via subcutaneous injection, typically once daily at bedtime. This timing maximizes effect by aligning with the body's natural nighttime GH release. The needles are small — insulin gauge — and most patients become comfortable with the process quickly.

Can I combine sermorelin with other peptides?

Yes. Sermorelin is commonly combined with Ipamorelin or CJC-1295 for synergistic GH stimulation. It can also be used alongside BPC-157 for recovery support or NAD+ for broader anti-aging effects.

Is there an age limit for sermorelin therapy?

Sermorelin is most effective when the pituitary gland is still capable of producing GH — which it is in most adults. It works best for adults aged 30–70. Results may be less dramatic in patients with severely compromised pituitary function.
